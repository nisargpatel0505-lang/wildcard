How to Play

You hold 9 cards. Select up to 5, then Play to score them or Discard to redraw. One 52-card deck per Heat — binned cards stay gone until the next Heat.
Score = (Hand Base + rank sum ×0.6) × Multiplier. Only cards forming the hand score — a Pair scores 2 cards, kickers score nothing. Ace is always 15. Tap ♠ Hands in-game for the full table.
Each Heat: a target, 4 plays, 5 discards. Every 3rd Heat (and all Endless Heats) twists the rules with a Heat Modifier. Heat 12 is the boss — THE HOUSE jams joker ×Mult (enhanced cards still work).
Shops sell jokers (max 1 per shop, 5 slots, swap when buying at full slots, sell in any shop) and supplies: destroy, copy or re-suit deck cards, and Hand Boosts that permanently level up a hand type's base value.
Money earns interest: +1 run coin per 5 held when you clear a Heat (max +5). Spend or compound — your call. Reroll the joker offer for 2 coins.
Clearing a Heat in fewer plays earns a better grade: S (+3 coins), A (+2), B (+1), C. Top 5 runs make the high score board.
Use the Scoring speed setting for Normal or Fast pacing. Tap 🃏 Deck to inspect your sculpted deck — supply changes join the deck from the next Heat.
Clear Heat 12 to win, then go Endless (Heat 13+). Higher Heats pay more run and account coins — spend account coins on permanent joker unlocks.
The Enhancer supply etches a card permanently: Gilded +8 rank · Neon +0.20 Mult · Glass ×1.5 Mult but may shatter · Wild counts as any suit for Flushes.
Between runs: Weekly Missions and the one-attempt Daily Challenge pay account coins, the Cabinet tracks badges and titles, and Sly's Stake Contract lets you back yourself from the Start Boost screen.
